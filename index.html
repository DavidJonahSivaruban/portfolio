About Me

I'm a UI Designer/Frontend Developer based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

I graduated from Taylor's University with a Bachelor's degree in Accounting and bounced through the next 4.5 years across recruitment and sales without a clue on what path I wanted to take, all I knew is that I wanted to build and create.

Feeling demotivated and a lil' bit lost throughout my career as time went by pushed me to try my hand at programming (coding was always a daunting field to me but, meh, what the hell, why not!).

so, that I did! I quit my job and joined a bootcamp focused on fullstack web development and haven't looked back since. When i'm not coding the matrix, I bake pastries and play music with my guitar.
